Ingredients

  • 3 large onions
  • 2 large potatoes
  • 2 medium carrots
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 185 grams of Java curry sauce
  • 800ml of water
  • ~350 grams of rice
  1. Finely chop the onions
  2. Peel the potatoes
  3. Chop the carrots
  4. Add oil to a pot and heat up
  5. Add the onion and cook until translucent
  6. Add the potatoes and carrots, cook for another 3 minutes
  7. Add the water, cook until it starts to boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 7 minutes
  8. Break up the curry roux in small pieces and add to the pot. Stir gently to blend the roux with the water
  9. Place a lid on the pot and cook for 10 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally
  10. Cook the rice
  11. Add the rice to the bowl, spoon over the curry and enjoy!
